Step by Step Guide

How to join the test.

Follow these steps carefully. It takes less than 2 minutes and you only need to do this once per app.

1
Open Google Chrome and sign in with your Gmail

On your Android phone, open Google Chrome. Make sure you are signed in with the Gmail account you registered as a tester. The testing access is tied to your Google account- use the exact Gmail you gave to the developer.

2
Copy the testing link of the app you want

Scroll down to the apps section below. Find the app you want to test and tap the "Copy Link" button. The link will be copied to your clipboard automatically.

📋 One tap to copy — no typing needed
3
Paste the link in Google Chrome's search bar

Go to Google Chrome on your Android phone. Tap the address bar at the top, then long-press and tap "Paste". Press Enter to open the link. This will take you directly to the Google Play testing page.

📱 Make sure Chrome is signed in to your tester Gmail
4
Tap "Become a tester" on the Google Play page

On the page that opens, you will see a button that says "Become a tester". Tap it and wait a few seconds. Once confirmed, the page will refresh and show an Install button. You only need to do this once.

5
Tap "Install" to download the app

After becoming a tester, tap "Install". The app will download and install normally on your phone, just like any other app from the Play Store.

⏱ Installation takes about 30 to 60 seconds
6
Explore the app and send your feedback

Use the app normally. Try every button, every feature, every screen. If something crashes, freezes, looks wrong, or feels confusin- take a screenshot and send it to the developer. Send all your feedback to aronmickoboquiren@gmail.com.

💬 Honest feedback = better app for everyone
