Our Story

Why these apps exist.

I know what anxiety feels like. I know what it is like to wake up with your heart racing, not knowing why. I know what panic attacks feel like in the middle of the day when everything seems fine on the outside.

These apps were not built in a big office by a large team. They were built by one person, with a laptop, a lot of prayer, and a genuine desire to help people feel less alone in what they are going through.

If even one person opens Serevibe or Serenara on their worst day and feels a little more peace, that is enough for me.

Aron Micko Boquiren   Founder · Aron Micko Labs
