Step by Step Guide

How to join the test.

Follow these steps carefully. It takes less than 2 minutes and you only need to do this once per app.

1
Sign in to Google Play with your Gmail

Open the Google Play Store on your Android phone and make sure you are signed in with your Gmail account. The testing access is tied to your Google account, so use the exact Gmail you registered with the developer.

2
Tap the testing button for the app you want

Scroll down to the apps section below. Find the app you want to test and tap the big green button. It will open that app's Google Play testing page directly in your browser or Play Store.

📱 Open these links on your Android phone
3
Tap "Become a tester" on the Google Play page

On the page that opens, you will see a button that says "Become a tester". Tap it and wait a few seconds. Once confirmed, the page will refresh and show an Install button. You only need to do this once.

4
Tap "Install" or "Download on Google Play"

After becoming a tester, tap "Install". The app will download and install normally on your phone, just like any other app from the Play Store.

⏱ Installation takes about 30 to 60 seconds
5
Explore the app and note anything unusual

Use the app normally. Try every button, every feature, every screen. If something crashes, freezes, looks wrong, or feels confusing — take a screenshot and send it to the developer. Every report matters, no matter how small it seems.

6
Send your feedback via email

When you have tested the app, send your honest feedback to aronmickoboquiren@gmail.com. Screenshots, bug descriptions, feature ideas, and even things you loved — all of it is welcome. No feedback is too small.

💬 Honest feedback = better app for everyone
