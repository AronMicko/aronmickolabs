Step by Step Guide

How to join the test.

Follow these steps carefully. It takes less than 2 minutes and you only need to do this once per app.

1
Sign in to Google Play with your Gmail

Make sure you are signed in to the Google Play Store app on your Android phone using your Gmail account. The testing link is tied to your Google account, so you must use the same Gmail you gave to the developer.

2
Tap the app's testing link below

Scroll down and find the app you want to test. Tap the big green "Become a Tester" button for that app. This will open the Google Play testing page directly.

📱 Open on your Android phone for best results
3
Tap "Become a tester"

On the Google Play page, you will see a button that says "Become a tester". Tap it and wait a few seconds for it to confirm. You only need to do this once.

4
Install the app

After becoming a tester, tap "Download on Google Play" or "Install". The app will install normally on your phone just like any other app from the Play Store.

⏱ Installation takes about 30 seconds
5
Use the app and note any issues

Explore all the features. Try everything. If anything crashes, feels off, or could be improved, take note or screenshot it and send your feedback to the developer. Every report helps.
